This Slack application adds the slash-command /news to your Slack. When a user enters that command, the app will offer a list of possible news sources. Select one to receive the latest headlines. (Once installed, type /news help for a couple of additional shortcuts.)

Privacy policy

This app collects minimal personal information from Slack (if any). We won't use or share such information in any context.
