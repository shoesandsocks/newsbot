Need some news in your Slack?

This app has two core features:

It add the slash-command /news to your Slack. When a user enters that command, the app will offer a list of possible news sources. Select one to receive the latest headlines.

It adds a bot to your team that can send selected headlines to you. Open a DM with @newsbot and type help to get started. Or skip the help and type schedule to jump right in. Other helpful terms include change, cancel, and remind me.

Privacy policy

This app collects minimal personal information from Slack (if any). We won't use or share such information in any context.
